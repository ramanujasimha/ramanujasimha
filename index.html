Hello! I am Ramanuja, a post-doctoral researcher at the University of Toronto / UHN, Canada. My research interests lie in machine learning, graphical modeling, data mining, and computational biology. Some of the topics I have worked on include multi-label classification and social network analysis.

I graduated with a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Delaware, USA in August 2016. For my PhD, I developed machine learning tools for protein multi-location prediction as well as for the more general problem of multi-label classification.

My graduate experience includes internships at GE Global Research in Niskayuna NY, LinkedIn in Mountain View CA, and National Center for Atmospheric Research in Boulder CO, and summer research at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh PA.

I got my Masters in Computer Science from the University of South Florida. Prior to my graduate studies, I worked as a software engineer at Tesco in the UK and in India. Even before that, I received my BE in Information Science from the National Institute of Engineering.
